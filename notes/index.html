About Notes

Notes are a sort of micro-blog for me. I aim to POSSE these to related websites if that is possible. You can expect longer replies to posts I find online, or replies containing stuff which I could consider to be necessary to be archived.

Indivivual notes's file names are in the format %Y-%V-nn.md, where %Y = full year, %V = ISO week number, nn = nth post for said week.
